# Nuts and Bolts

Sensory Bridge uses M3 screws and nuts to stay together:

| Product              | M3 Nut     | M3 Screw (6mm)    | M3 Screw (12mm)  |
|:---------------------|:-----------|:------------------|:-----------------|
| **Sensory Bridge**   | 4          | 4                 | 0                |
| **Mini Mast**        | 2          | 2                 | 2                |
